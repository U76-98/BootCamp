Soffy

Type - Bolognese

The Bolognese has a distinctive single coat (no undercoat) that falls in loose ringlets all over the body. The hair's texture is woolly, not silky, and requires regular combing to prevent matting. They are known for their playful and affectionate nature.

Max

Type - Bernese Mountain Dog

The Bernese Mountain Dog, or Berner Sennenhund, is a large breed from the Swiss Alps. They are a "Sennenhund-type" breed with roots in Roman mastiffs. Historically, they were used as general farm dogs and to pull carts as draft animals, making them strong and versatile companions.

Jimmy

Type - Irish Wolfhound

The Irish Wolfhound is known for its massive size. It is more heavily built than a Scottish Deerhound but less so than a Great Dane. Minimum heights can be up to 32 inches at the shoulder for males, making them one of the tallest dog breeds in the world.

Anku

Type - Volpino Italiano

The Volpino Italiano is a small dog with a long coat that stands away from its body. The coat is typically white, deep red, or black. They are known to be vigilant and energetic, making them excellent watchdogs despite their small size.

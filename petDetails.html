About Anku

The Volpino Italiano, or "Italian Spitz," is a small, energetic dog with roots tracing back to ancient Italy. Known for their thick, white coat and vigilant nature, they are highly intelligent and very attached to their families. Originally used as watchdogs, they are loyal and playful companions.
